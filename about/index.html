electionmap.lol v2.1.1 | 02/17/25

We sure do hope there are future elections we can use this for. Primaries, pollster analysis, governor map, coming eventually.

ElectionMap.lol hosts a model and visualization created in August 2024 by jessebeach50 and tbjones1612. The site was moved to React after the 2024 elections in November, and ultimately switched back off of React in January 2025. Github repository is available here.

Our model was, much to our surprise, extremely accurate in predicting the 2024 elections. Every state swayed correctly in the Presidential, one Senate race was wrong (PA), and predicted 420 out of 435 House races correctly.
