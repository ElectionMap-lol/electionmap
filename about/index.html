electionmap.lol v2.4.1 | 07/24/25

ElectionMap.lol hosts a model and visualization created in August 2024.

Github Repository

Our model was, much to our surprise, extremely accurate in predicting the 2024 elections. Every state swayed correctly in the Presidential, one Senate race was wrong (PA), and predicted 420 out of 435 House races correctly. Other models were added after the 2024 elections, and our old models were updated as well. Poll analysis page planned for early 2026.
